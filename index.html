No. You only need to explain the task you currently do manually.

You can. For simple one-off tasks, you probably should. I help when you need a repeatable workflow that works with your real files, edge cases, checks, and business rules.

Yes. Most projects are fully remote via video call and file sharing. That keeps the process fast, simple, and cost-effective. For larger projects in Switzerland, on-site sessions may be possible by agreement.

Depending on the task: Excel, Google Sheets, Python, Apps Script, simple dashboards, and AI-ready summaries.

No. This is for small, practical automations. Larger or more complex projects are possible — we scope them case by case.

I will tell you honestly and suggest a smaller first version or a diagnosis before building.
