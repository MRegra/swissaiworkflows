Why not just use AI yourself?

You can. For simple one-off tasks, you probably should.

But most business workflows are not one clean prompt. They involve messy spreadsheets, changing file formats, missing data, business rules, VAT, reporting logic, and people who need to reuse the process later.

AI can generate pieces. I turn those pieces into a working workflow: cleaned data, tested calculations, error checks, documentation, and a short handover video.

You are not paying for AI. You are paying to get the task solved and off your plate.

Before
